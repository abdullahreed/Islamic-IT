A Reminder From Surah Al-Hashr

Surah Al-Hashr of the Qur'an includes a valuable lesson.
It's about the weakness of material wealth.
It's relevant here because current IT requires not only ingenuity for creating and using software, but also expensive precision-manufactured materials for hardware.
To get the full lesson, read the surah.
We should all read it and learn it well,
إن شاء الله .
However, if in the meantime, you need a quicker reminder then here are the last two lines of the surah.


Ayah 23

هُوَ اللَّهُ الَّذِي لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ۖ الْمَلِكُ الْقُدُّوسُ السَّلَامُ الْمُؤْمِنُ الْمُهَيْمِنُ الْعَزِيزُ الْجَبَّارُ الْمُتَكَبِّرُ ۚ سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ عَمَّا يُشْرِكُونَ

He is God, there is no deity except Him, The Sovereign, The Holy, The Source of Peace, The Granter of Faith, The Overseer, The Almighty, The Compeller, The Supreme in Greatness. Glory be to God, free from what they associate.

Ayah 24

هُوَ اللَّهُ الْخَالِقُ الْبَارِئُ الْمُصَوِّرُ لَهُ الْأَسْمَاءُ الْحُسْنَىٰ ۚ يُسَبِّحُ لَهُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۖ وَهُوَ الْعَزِيزُ الْحَكِيمُ

He is God, The Creator, The Evolver, The Shaper. To Him belong the most beautiful names. Everything in the heavens and the earth glorifies Him, and He is the Almighty, the All-Wise.
